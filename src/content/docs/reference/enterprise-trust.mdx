AI Foundation Services is built on sovereign European infrastructure with enterprise-grade security. Your data stays in Europe, is never used for model training, and is protected by German and EU data protection law.


## Data Sovereignty

- **T-Cloud models** — Open-source models (Llama, Mistral, DeepSeek, Qwen, etc.) run on T-Systems infrastructure in Germany. Data is processed and stored exclusively in German data centers.
- **Hyperscaler models** — Closed-source models (GPT, Claude, Gemini) run on Azure, AWS, or Google Cloud within the EU. Data does not leave the European Union.
- **No US data transfer** — Regardless of model choice, no customer data is transferred outside the EU.


## Certifications

T-Systems operations are **ISO 27001** certified, covering information security management for the infrastructure and processes underlying AI Foundation Services.

For the complete list of certifications applicable to T-Cloud infrastructure, see the [T-Systems Certificates](https://www.t-systems.com/de/en/company/about-t-systems/t-systems-certificates) page.

:::note
Certification scope depends on the specific service and infrastructure layer. For questions about which certifications apply to your use case, contact the AIFS team.
:::


## GDPR Compliance

- AI Foundation Services is fully **GDPR compliant** under EU Regulation 2016/679
- Data processing agreements (DPA) are available on request for enterprise customers
- T-Systems acts as a data processor; you remain the data controller
- Legal basis, data subject rights, and processing records are documented in the DPA
